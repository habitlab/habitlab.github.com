HabitLab

Habitlab is a browser extension for improving your online habits using self-experimentation.

How it Works:

Set goals for websites you want to spend less time on.

  • Goal-Oriented
  • Large Library of Interventions
  • Open-Source and Extensible

HabitLab will try to find out which interventions work out for you.

  • Smartly Auto-Managed Options
  • User-Overridable
  • Completely Anonymous

View your progress on your goals and earn badges for achieving them.

  • Fully-Featured
  • Crowdsourced and Research-Driven
  • Proven Effective

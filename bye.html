Here are some possible reasons for uninstalling and how to fix them:

Disabling Annoying Interventions

You can disable interventions on the Settings page, which you can access by clicking the icon in the Chrome toolbar and clicking "Settings".

You can also disable the current intervention by clicking the icon in the Chrome toolbar and clicking "Disable Intervention".

Temporarily Disabling All Interventions

You can disable HabitLab for the rest of the day by clicking the icon in the Chrome toolbar, and checking "Disable HabitLab Today".

No Interventions Are Shown

Go to the Settings page and make sure that you have enabled that site on your goals list. You can go to the Settings page by clicking the icon in the Chrome toolbar and clicking "Disable Intervention".

Privacy / Security Concerns

You can disable logging on the Settings page. Also see our Privacy and security page

If you ever want to reinstall, visit https://habitlab.stanford.edu/
